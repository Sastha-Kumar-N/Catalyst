Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam Young Research Fellowhip

In memory of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, TERRE Policy Centre established the Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Young Research Fellowship to honor young Indian researchers with exceptional creativity and contributions in environmental protection. This fellowship, fully funded by TERRE, is awarded annually to those recognized for their outstanding research conducted in India.
